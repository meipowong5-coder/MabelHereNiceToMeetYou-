City University of Hong Kong
Workshop Introduction: The Jockey Club Arts-Tech Inclusion Project (Project IDEA) is funded by The Hong Kong Jockey Club Charities Trust. We help secondary school students and teachers discover arts technology through hands-on workshops, combining digital tools with creative expression while promoting inclusive art awareness. Movemin — Build Your Own Contactless Musical Instrument Inspired by the Theremin, students create their own infrared-sensor instrument (Movemin) using Arduino. They explore new ways of playing music with body movements or assistive tools, making musical expression truly accessible to everyone.

Magic Inclusive Project Pilot Incentive Scheme
Led a team that guided five secondary students from Cheung Sha Wan Catholic Secondary School in a sensory exploration of Sham Shui Po. Through guided sound walks and field recording, students created personal “memory maps”. All maps were merged into a large public collage map with QR codes linking to their original audio recordings — turning the neighbourhood into an inclusive, interactive sound experience.
